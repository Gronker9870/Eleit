Controlling a Buzzer With a Potentiometer

Here's a really simple project you can make regardless of skill level. All you need is an Arduino, breadboard, potentiometer, buzzer and jumper wires. Turning the potentiometer will change the pitch of the buzzer.

Creating the circuit

Skip to schematics Firstly, connect the power and ground pins on the Arduino to the breadboard, make sure your Arduino is not connected to power.
